# Inference Engineering Worklog

A learning-in-public workspace for the inference-engineer mission (see `MISSION.md`). It holds the private record of what has been learned and measured, and the public artifacts derived from it that get published to **resolute.dev**.

## Language

### Artifacts

**Worklog post**:
A public, first-person write-up of a result you measured yourself ("I predicted 27 tok/s, measured 24 — here's why"). The hero content of resolute.dev. Always *derived* from a learning record, never the record itself.
_Avoid_: blog post, article, entry

**Learning record** (`learning-records/`):
A private, third-person log of what you mastered, with the evidence and the pedagogical state (what's owned, what's next). Source material for a worklog post; never published as-is.
_Avoid_: journal, post, log entry

**Lesson** (`lessons/`):
A public, second-person teaching page that explains one concept the way you learned it (predict-then-measure). Supporting material a worklog post can assume.
_Avoid_: tutorial, post, guide

**Reference** (`reference/`):
A public, evergreen support page that is looked up rather than read start-to-finish (the learning path, the performance glossary).
_Avoid_: doc, page

### Site

**resolute.dev**:
The public site. Publishes worklog posts (hero), lessons and references (supporting). Everything private (learning records, mission, teaching notes) stays out of it.
_Avoid_: the blog, the website

## Public / private boundary

- **Public** (lives on resolute.dev): worklog posts, lessons, references.
- **Private** (never published): learning records, `MISSION.md`, `NOTES.md`.
- A public artifact is always a *deliberate derivation* of private material, never the private file made visible.
